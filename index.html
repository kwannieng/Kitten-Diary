My Kitten's Diary

1st Month

Kitten starts openning their eyes and ears. The blue eyes that kittens are born with may start to change color.

2nd Month

The first round of vaccinations should be done between six and eight weeks on the kitten timeline.

3rd Month

Kittens are ready to receive their first rabies vaccination by twelve weeks and finish the transition to solid food.

4th Month

Kittens' adult teeth will start to come in by their 4th month while their baby teeth start coming in during the 2nd week.

5th Month

Beginning to see and use ranking (dominant and submissive) within the household, including humans.

6th Month

Kittens are ready to be spayed or neutered by six months of age. And you can transition them to eating twice a day.

7th Month

Kittens are going through his teenage years and explore his independence, please give him plenty of love on his terms.

8th Month

Random Cat Fact: Cats are supposed to have 18 toes (five toes on each front paw; four toes on each back paw).

9th Month

Kittens are in their “teenage” phase, they are trying to hone their hunting skills.

10th Month

Random Cat Fact: Cats’ purring may be a self-soothing behavior, since they make this noise when they’re ill or distressed, as well as when they’re happy.

11th Month

Random Cat Fact: Cats’ claws all curve downward, which means that they can’t climb down trees head-first. Instead, they have to back down the trunk.

12th Month

Although a cat is considered an adult by one year old, developmentally speaking, kitten adolescence typically lasts until reaching eighteen months.
